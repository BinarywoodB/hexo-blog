## STRIDE
**STRIDE** represents these threat categories:
* **S**poofing (of user identity): 身份假冒
* **T**ampering: 篡改
* **R**epudiation: 抵赖
* **I**nformation Disclosure: 信息披露
* **D**enial of Service (DoS): 拒绝服务
* **E**levation of Privilege: 特权提升
